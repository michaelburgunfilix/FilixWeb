About Filix

Filix Pty Ltd was set up by Michael Burgun in 2006 when he moved his software business back to Australia.  Filix now operates from purpose-built offices just south of Coffs Harbour on the northern coast of New South Wales.  Coffs Harbour is an hour's flight from Sydney or Brisbane for easy international flight connections, enabling Filix to maintain close business relationships with clients in North America and Europe.
